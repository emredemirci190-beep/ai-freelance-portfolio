TechStore Product and Service Guide v1.0

SECTION 1 - RETURN POLICY
Products can be returned within 30 days of purchase.
Original invoice is required for returns. Bank statement is accepted if invoice is unavailable.
Products with physical damage are not eligible for return.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after return approval.

SECTION 2 - SHIPPING INFORMATION
Standard shipping takes 2-5 business days. Weekends are not counted.
Orders over 500 TL qualify for free shipping.
Shipment tracking is available via the link in the order confirmation email.
Address changes can be made before the order is dispatched.

SECTION 3 - WARRANTY CONDITIONS
All products come with a 2-year manufacturer warranty.
Warranty covers manufacturing defects and material faults.
Physical damage, water contact, and unauthorized repairs void the warranty.
Products must be brought in original packaging for warranty service.

SECTION 4 - PAYMENT OPTIONS
Installment plans available up to 12 months on credit cards.
3% discount applied for bank transfer payments.
Cash on delivery is available at no extra charge.
